Spotify Export Service

With this application you can download saved tracks and playlists from spotify user's library

Required Spotify access:

Application needs access to your Spotify account to see your library. Once you click on the "sign in with spotify" button, this service will redirect you to the Spotify Account service. If security check passes and access granted, this service gains your Spotify access token. Logging out deletes server-stored access token.
